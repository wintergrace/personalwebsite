About Me

My passion for learning was ignited at a very young age. Through homeschooling, I was able to enroll in community college at the age of 11. I learned calculus and fell in love with the sciences. My curiosity for the world carried me through to earning my Bachelor's at age 16. Post graduation, I've immersed myself in a new world: computer science. I love both the creative and optimization sides of coding, and the moment where everything comes together. I'm excited to pursue my career in this field.
When I'm not working, you'll find me reading, playing the viola, or playing video games with my friends. I'm an extrovert and generally spend as much time as I can with my people.
I think everyone has something unique to bring to the world, and I strongly affirm the unbelievable difference a single person can make. I want to work with a team of individuals who are motivated to solve problems and have fun along the way.
